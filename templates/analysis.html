75 PERCENT ACCURACY (TEST DATA)

Machine Learning using Neural Network & KNN

  • The team developed a machine learning model to predict if each player can earn avarage salary of 7 Milion in a year based on certain attributes
  • Attributes are - Age , # of games/year, Field goal %, 3 points %, 2 points %, # of Rebounds, # Assists, Point shots, Year,
  • At first we tried to predict the exact salary of the player but the models accuracy was very low, hence the output nodes were decreased to just two, will the player be a HERO by getting the Average salary or a ZREO?
  • The activation functions reLu, sigmoid, linear, and tanh all produced similar Accuracy results
  • As we adjusted the output nodes from 125 to 10 to 2 using reLu, we found that the Accuracy % increased due to the relatively limited output nodes.
  • We did not observe a change in the Accuracy upon increasing the number of rows with player data.
  • We also built a KNN classifier model that resulted in a similar accuracy of 74 % as our NN model.
  • Considering both NN and KNN models generated similar accuracy levels, we believe our NN model is robust and can be enhanced by adding more data or training it with a more comprehensive data set.
